## 2. Data Integrity and Ethical Use

1. **No Personal Identifiable Information (PII):**  
   The COCO dataset contains images of common scenes and objects and does not include identifiable personal data. Any additional custom dataset components will be screened to remove or blur faces and identifying features before use.

2. **Ethical Standards:**  
   - The dataset is used solely for **research and educational purposes** related to computer vision.  
   - The model will not be used to make decisions that affect individuals’ rights or access to resources.  
   - Any downstream usage will respect fairness, accountability, and transparency principles.

3. **Bias Awareness:**  
   The COCO dataset may contain natural imbalances in object types or scene representations. Efforts will be made to document and mitigate any potential biases during model evaluation and reporting.
